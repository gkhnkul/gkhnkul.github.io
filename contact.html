Prospective Students

I am always looking for motivated Ph.D. students with interests in cybersecurity, machine learning resilience, database systems, or software engineering tooling. I do not currently have immediate openings, but it can be helpful to start the conversation early — please email a brief note describing your interests and attach your CV.

Master's students (thesis or project track) interested in working with the lab should reach out as soon as possible — earlier in the semester is much easier than later.

UMassD undergraduates interested in research opportunities are also welcome to email; I take undergraduate researchers when projects and funding align.

Other Inquiries

For collaborations, speaking engagements, or media inquiries, email is the fastest way to reach me. For department-level inquiries, please contact the CIS Department directly.
